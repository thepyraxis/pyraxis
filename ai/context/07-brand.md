# 07 · Brand

## Purpose

Single reference for voice, tone, and messaging so any AI or writer produces on-brand copy without re-reading the full Brand Bible.

## Responsibilities

Governs all copywriting: navigation, headlines, CTAs, section content, error states, loading states.

## Core Principles

**Personality.** Intelligent, precise, confident, quiet, sophisticated, intentional. Visitors should feel "this company builds systems," never "this company sells websites." (Brand Bible §04)

**Positioning.** PYRAXIS sells business infrastructure / predictable growth / systems / outcomes — not websites / AI automation / services / features. (Brand Bible §05)

**Voice.** Intelligent, calm, confident, direct, minimal. Never friendly-casual, never corporate-hollow.

**Banned words:** innovative, cutting-edge, revolutionary, best-in-class, next-generation, world-class, amazing, seamless. Replace with concrete outcomes. (Brand Bible §06)

**Messaging formula for every section:** `Problem → Cost → Solution → Result`. Example: Ghosted Leads → Lost Revenue → Lead Engine → Qualified Customers. (Brand Bible §07)

**Writing rules:**
- Sentence length mix: 70% short, 20% medium, 10% long
- Break ideas across lines, one idea per line, e.g. "Never Miss / An Opportunity."
- Editing test for every sentence: "Can this be shorter?" If yes, rewrite.
- Never fake statistics, awards, testimonials, or case studies.
(Brand Bible §08)

**Navigation copy:** `Infrastructure · Blueprint · Deployments · Mission · Connect` — never "Services · About · Blog · Resources". (Brand Bible §09)

**CTA copy:** "Book a Discovery Call" not "Learn More"; "Start Building" not "Submit"; "Deploy Infrastructure" not "Click Here"; "Explore the System" not "Read More". (Brand Bible §10)

**Emotional architecture across the page:** Arrival → Curiosity → Problem → Discovery → Understanding → Trust → Confidence → Wonder → Decision → Calm. Per-scene emotional targets are listed in `ai/knowledge/sections.json`. (Brand Bible §11)

**Content hierarchy.** Every screen: one idea. Visitor must understand Who/What/Why/How/Action within 5 seconds. Section formula: one headline → one sentence → three bullets → one visual → one CTA. Full homepage reading time target: 3–5 minutes. (Brand Bible §12)

**Three-Act narrative:**
- Act I — Recognition: Arrival → Curiosity → Recognition → Hope
- Act II — Infrastructure: Understanding → Capability → Proof → Trust
- Act III — Decision: Trust → Future → Decision → Calm
(Brand Bible §13)
