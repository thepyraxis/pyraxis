```md
- **Outcome:** the concrete result the user/business gets, not the mechanism
- **Which Growth Engine (if any):** Lead | Booking | Follow-up | Retention | Review | Referral | Intelligence | N/A
- **Which scene(s) it appears in:** 
- **Design tokens/surfaces used:** 
- **Motion behavior:** construct/manufacture/flow/morph/merge-split/assemble-disassemble (never fade/pop/slide/zoom/bounce)
- **Accessibility considerations:** 
- **Status:** not-started | in-progress | complete
```
